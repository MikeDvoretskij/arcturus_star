Nord GSM Metal

Description

Nord GSM Metal, which is a hybrid security and fire control panel designed to protect large offices, shops, banks, and suburban real estate. It features GSM, Ethernet, and radio channels, two sim-cards, and a GSM antenna, as well as a built-in power supply unit and accommodates a battery of 7.2 A*h. The panel can accommodate up to 8 (16) wired security zones, 2 wire fire zones, 7 discrete outputs, and up to 31 wireless devices. It comes in a metal housing that allows the use of a radio channel to deliver messages to a security console. The panel can be configured easily using a special software called Hubble configurator, which allows the engineer to connect a laptop via USB and configure the device's communication with the console, partitions, users, sensors, and other parameters of the alarm system. The Nord GSM Metal also offers automation, fire alarm, and safety and comfort features for users of the security alarm system.

Technical specifications

Connection Channels:
GSM: 2 SIM-cards, internal GSM-antenna, remote GSM-antenna (optional).
Ethernet: Option with Ethernet adapter.
Radio Channel: Option with radio transmitters TP-100-H Low and TP-100-H High.
Wireless Devices:31 devices: security sensors, utility detectors (smoke, water, and temperature), repeaters, extra keypad, and relay.
Inputs and Outputs:8 (16) wired zones: expansion with EW-12.2 fire zones: expansion with EF-2 to create 2 fire zones from 2 security zones.
7 discrete outputs: TP100-OUT connector.
1-Wire: Connect TM or RFID-reader, light alarm, siren, and remote temperature sensor.
Users and Partitions:32 TM or RFID-keys.
32 partitions.Housing:295x258x81 mm plastic housing.
Power Supply:
Principal: Remote power supply from the network 110/220 V (1.5 A, 12 V).
Backup: Accommodates battery 7.2 A*h, 12 V.
Temperature Range:-25…+50˚C.
Configuration:
Mini USB (Hubble Configurator).
Virtual Expert App (Hubble Configurator).
Bluetooth (coming soon).
Supply Kit:
