Nord Mini

Description

The Nord Mini is a wired control panel suitable for small offices and commercial premises with a low budget. It has a built-in keypad and is expandable up to 16 zones or discrete outputs with an external expander. The Nord Mini is easy to install and comes with a remote power supply unit, battery, and control devices. The Nord GSM Mini, PIR, magnetic contact, and MyAlarm App can be added to create a wired security system. The Hubble configurator makes configuration easy, and the device can be tested through a convenient web app. The panel can be remotely serviced and updated through the cloud interface, and it is protected from hijacking by competitors with a complex password and connection to the console. The Nord Mini can prevent inference panel fail with the "Probable alarm" event and works with Security Center, one of the best professional consoles in Russia. The MyAlarm App has a custom event timeline, temperature control, and automation control features.
