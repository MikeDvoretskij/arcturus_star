Nord Pro Air

Description

Nord GSM Air, a wireless control panel with a built-in keypad suitable for low-budget security systems in small offices and flats. The device can connect to up to 31 wireless devices such as security sensors, smoke detectors, water leak sensors, temperature sensors, relays, and repeaters. It has two discrete outputs for connection to wired sirens or light alarms and two wired zones without power supply. The device has a backup power supply of 1.1 A*h, 3.7 V, lasting for at least 12 hours. The device is easy to configure and comes with a special software called Hubble that the engineer uses to connect the laptop to the Nord device and configure its communication with consoles, partitions, users, sensors, and other parameters of the alarm system. The device has a status panel to check the sensors' and other devices' settings, and the engineer can test the connection channels using a convenient web app. The device has won awards for innovation and has distant services for remote access by an engineer to configure the device without visiting the site. The firmware of the control panel and other devices can be remotely updated, and the device has protection against hijacking by competitors. The text also describes how the device prevents inference panel fail during entry delay, has a personal account and app called MyAlarm for the client of a private security company to control the security system and video surveillance. Finally, the device works with one of the best professional consoles in Russia, the Security Center.
