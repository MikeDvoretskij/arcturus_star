Nord GSM

Description

Nord GSM, a hybrid security and fire control panel designed for professional protection of commercial sites such as offices, restaurants, shops, and ATMs. The panel has a compact housing made of quality plastic and features 8 (16) security zones, 2 fire zones, and 7 discrete outputs. It can support up to 31 wireless devices using the wireless expander CN-WRL and has a built-in power supply unit from the network with a backup power supply of a battery of 1.2 Ah or 2.2 Ah. Nord GSM has two SIM-card slots and supports GSM and Ethernet channels for sending messages to the security console. It also offers an easy installation process and a convenient web app for testing connection channels. Additionally, Nord GSM has automation features such as arming according to schedule, light alarm for partition, and control with MyAlarm. It comes with all the necessary equipment for installation of security and fire alarm systems, including fire detectors, sirens, fire buttons, and a sign "Exit." Furthermore, Nord GSM is designed to meet the technical requirements of banks and can be used for ATM security. It also offers remote arming and disarming from the console, making it a popular choice among banks.

Technical specifications

Connection Channels:
GSM: 2 sim-cards, Internal GSM-antenna, Remote GSM-antenna (option)
Ethernet: Option with Ethernet Adapter
Wireless Devices:
Supports up to 31 wireless devices including security sensors, utility detectors (smoke, water, temperature), repeaters, extra keypad, and relay.
Inputs and Outputs:
8 (16) wired zones with expansion capability with EW-12.
2 fire zones with expansion capability with EF-2.
7 discrete outputs.
1-Wire interface to connect TM or RFID-reader, light alarm, siren, and remote temperature sensor.
Mini USB port for configuration.
Users and Partitions:
Supports up to 32 users with TM or RFID-keys.
Supports up to 32 partitions.
Housing:
Plastic housing with dimensions of 200x186x61 mm.
Power Supply:
Principal power supply is remote from the network 110/220 V (1.5 A, 12 V).
Backup power supply is a battery of 1.2A*h, 12V.
Temperature Range:
Operating temperature range is -25…+50˚ C.
Configuration:
Configuration can be done through Mini USB using Hubble Configurator.
Virtual Expert App (Hubble configurator) can also be used for configuration.
Bluetooth configuration will be available soon.
Supply Kit:
The Nord GSM Security System comes with a built-in power supply unit from the 110/220 V network and built-in GSM-antenna.
